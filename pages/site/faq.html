Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Poornimites

🔐 Getting Started

Do I need an account to use Poornimites?

Most features are accessible without an account! However, creating an account allows you to save your preferences, contribute to discussions, upload resources, and access personalized features like your dashboard.

Is Poornimites only for Poornima College students?

While designed primarily for Poornima College students, many tools like the CGPA calculator, converters, and productivity tools can be used by anyone. Community features and college-specific resources are tailored for our students.

🛠️ Features & Tools

What tools are available?

We offer a wide range of tools including CGPA calculators, timetable managers, Pomodoro timers, PDF tools, unit converters, developer tools, note-taking workspace, and much more. Check out our Tools page for the complete list.

Can I suggest new features?

Absolutely! We're always looking to improve. You can reach out through our contact form, email us, or post your suggestions in the community forums. Student feedback drives our development.

📚 Resources & Notes

How do I upload/download notes?

Navigate to the Notes & Resources section, select your semester and subject, and you can browse available materials. To upload, you'll need to be logged in with a verified student account.

Are the notes verified or moderated?

We rely on community moderation and student ratings. Popular and helpful resources rise to the top, while inappropriate content can be reported and will be reviewed by our moderation team.

👥 Community & Privacy

Is my data safe?

Yes! We take privacy seriously. We don't sell your data, and we use industry-standard security measures. Check our Privacy Policy for detailed information.

Can I participate anonymously?

Certain features like confessions and polls allow anonymous participation. However, uploading resources and some community features require a verified account for quality and safety reasons.

💻 Technical Support

The site isn't working properly. What should I do?

Try clearing your browser cache and cookies first. Make sure you're using an updated browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari recommended). If issues persist, email us at support@poornimites.com with details about the problem.

Is there a mobile app?

Currently, Poornimites is a web-based platform optimized for mobile browsers. A dedicated mobile app is on our roadmap for future development!

🤝 Contributing

Can I help develop Poornimites?

We welcome contributions from talented students! If you're interested in web development, design, content creation, or have other skills to offer, reach out to us. Check our Credits page to see our current team.
